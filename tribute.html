EMILIO PABLO ESCOBAR GAVIRIA

The King Of Cocaine and the founder of the Medellin Cartel

Here is a timeline for Escobar's life

  • 1949 - Born in Rionegro Colombia
  • 1970 - Escobar began his criminal career as a small-time thief and smuggler, transporting contraband such as cigarettes and electronics from neighboring countries into Colombia.
  • 1975 - Escobar reportedly established his own drug trafficking operation, smuggling cocaine into the United States through Panama.
  • 1976 - Escobar was arrested for drug trafficking in Ecuador but managed to escape from prison and return to Colombia.
  • 1980 - Escobar became the leader of the Medellin Cartel, one of the world's largest and most powerful drug trafficking organizations. He was responsible for the production and distribution of massive amounts of cocaine, which he sold in the United States and other countries.
  • 1982 - Escobar was elected to the Colombian Congress as an alternate member, although he never actually took his seat.
  • 1984 - Escobar's drug empire was estimated to be worth over $3 billion, making him one of the richest people in the world.
  • 1989 - Escobar ordered the bombing of a commercial airliner in an attempt to kill a presidential candidate who was running against him. The attack killed all 107 people on board.
  • 1991 - Escobar surrendered to the Colombian government and was imprisoned in a luxury prison he had built for himself. However, he continued to run his drug empire from behind bars.
  • 1992 - After Colombian authorities attempted to move him to a less comfortable prison, Escobar escaped and went into hiding.
  • 1993 - Escobar was finally located and killed by Colombian security forces in a shootout in Medellin. He was 44 years old.
